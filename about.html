About Naruto Shippuden

Naruto Shippuden takes place after Naruto comes back from a training trip with Jiraiya, one of three legendary Sannin. He takes Naruto in as a request of Naruto's late father, Minato Namikaze, the Hidden Leaf's Village Fourth Hokage.
